# 🎓 BSc Nutrition Courses at Besant Women’s College

## 1. BSc Food, Nutrition & Dietetics (FND)

### 📚 Course Overview

* **Duration:** 3 years (6 semesters) ([Shiksha][1])
* **Mode:** Full-time
* **Affiliation:** Mangalore University ([Careers360][2])
* **Intake:** ~60 seats ([Shiksha][1])

---

### 📖 What You Study

Core subjects include:

* Human Nutrition
* Food Science
* Dietetics (clinical nutrition)
* Biochemistry
* Community Nutrition
* Food Preservation & Safety

The course is handled under the **Department of Food, Nutrition & Dietetics / Home Science** ([bwc.besant.edu.in][3])

---

### 🎯 Eligibility

* Pass in **PUC / 12th (any stream with Chemistry preferred)** ([Careers360][2])
* Admission is usually **merit-based (marks)**

---

### 💰 Fees (Very Affordable)

* **Total course fee:** ~₹18,000 – ₹22,500 for 3 years ([Shiksha][4])
  👉 That’s roughly **₹6k–₹8k per year**

This makes it one of the **cheapest nutrition degrees in Karnataka**.

---

### 🏫 Facilities & Exposure

* Nutrition labs & practical sessions
* Field visits (community nutrition programs)
* Internships & projects in food/nutrition sector ([Shiksha][5])

---

## 2. BSc Home Science (Alternative Path)

If you want a **broader approach**, this is also available:

* **Duration:** 3 years ([Careers360][6])
* Includes **Nutrition + Family Studies + Human Development**

👉 Good option if you want flexibility in career.

---

## 3. MSc Food Science & Nutrition (After BSc)

* **Duration:** 2 years
* Advanced specialization in:

  * Clinical nutrition
  * Food science research
  * Public health nutrition ([bwc.besant.edu.in][7])

---

# 🧾 Simple Comparison

| Course                         | Best For                       | Fees       |
| ------------------------------ | ------------------------------ | ---------- |
| BSc Food Nutrition & Dietetics | Dietitian / Nutritionist       | ⭐ Very Low |
| BSc Home Science               | Broad career options           | Low        |
| MSc Nutrition                  | Specialization & higher salary | Moderate   |

---

# ✅ Final Guidance

👉 Choose **BSc Food, Nutrition & Dietetics** if your goal is:

* Clinical dietitian
* Nutrition consultant
* Health & wellness expert

👉 Choose **Home Science** if:

* You want **multiple career options** (teaching, nutrition, childcare, etc.)

---

💡 Honest advice:
This college is **excellent for low-budget students**, but if you want **premium hospital exposure**, you may compare with Manipal/Yenepoya.
