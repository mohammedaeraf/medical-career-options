# Paramedical Courses in India that do not require NEET

Many **paramedical courses in India do not require the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test**. Admission is usually based on **12th marks (PCB or PCMB)** or a **college entrance test**. These courses are good options for students who want to work in the healthcare field without becoming doctors.

Below are some of the **best paramedical courses without NEET**.
